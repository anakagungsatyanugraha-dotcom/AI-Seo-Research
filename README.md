# AI SEO Research

This repository collects research on AI-powered SEO content production for B2B SaaS. It serves as a central workspace for gathering insights, analyzing trends, and documenting findings related to how artificial intelligence is transforming search engine optimization and content strategy in the B2B software market.

## Project Structure

```
research/
├── sources.md
├── linkedin-posts/
├── youtube-transcripts/
└── other/
```

- **sources.md** — Index of research sources and references
- **linkedin-posts/** — Saved LinkedIn posts and related content
- **youtube-transcripts/** — Transcripts from relevant YouTube videos
- **other/** — Additional research materials
