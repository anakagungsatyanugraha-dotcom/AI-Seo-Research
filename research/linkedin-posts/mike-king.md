# Mike King

## Post 1

**Date:** Approximately 2 weeks ago

**Topic:** SEO remains fundamental in the age of AI Search

### Summary

Mike King shares an interview discussing how AI search systems are built upon Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), which combines large language models with traditional search engines. He explains that SEO remains essential because AI systems rely on high-quality indexed web content to retrieve information before generating answers. Improving a brand's visibility across the web increases its likelihood of being referenced by AI-powered search systems.

### Key Takeaways

- AI search builds on traditional search rather than replacing it.
- Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) depends on strong SEO foundations.
- Website authority and broad web visibility improve AI citations.
- GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) extends traditional SEO practices.
- Organizations should optimize for both search engines and AI assistants.

### Why it matters for AI-powered SEO content production

This post reinforces that AI search optimization does not replace SEO but expands upon it. B2B SaaS companies should continue investing in authoritative content, technical SEO, and brand visibility to improve the chances of being retrieved and cited by AI systems.

## Post 2

**Date:** Approximately 1 week ago

**Topic:** AI search and the decline of critical thinking

### Summary

Mike King reflects on one of the unintended consequences of AI-powered search: users increasingly receive direct answers instead of exploring multiple sources. He argues that traditional search encouraged people to compare viewpoints and evaluate evidence, whereas AI assistants often summarize information into a single response. While AI greatly improves convenience, it may also reduce users' willingness to critically evaluate information.

### Key Takeaways

- AI search prioritizes convenience over exploration.
- Traditional search encouraged comparison across multiple sources.
- Overreliance on AI summaries may reduce critical thinking.
- Human judgment remains important when interpreting AI-generated information.
- AI search should complement—not replace—independent research.

### Why it matters for AI-powered SEO content production

This post highlights an important consideration for AI-powered SEO: producing trustworthy, evidence-based content becomes even more valuable when AI systems summarize information for users. High-quality, well-supported content helps ensure that AI-generated answers remain accurate and reliable.
