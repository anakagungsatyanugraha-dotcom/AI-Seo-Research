## Post 1

**Date:** Approximately 19 hours ago

**Topic:** Affiliate SEO is still growing despite AI Overviews

### Summary

Aleyda Solis analyzes affiliate websites such as RunRepeat, Pack Hacker, and CleverHiker to examine how AI Overviews affect organic traffic. Despite AI Overviews appearing on 81–95% of their top non-branded queries, these websites continue to grow year over year.

She argues that AI Overviews replace simple informational searches but are much less effective for purchase-decision queries where users seek detailed comparisons, testing, reviews, and real-world experience. Successful affiliate sites continue to win by publishing original research, first-hand testing, strong comparisons, and building recognizable brands.

### Key Takeaways

- AI Overviews appear on most commercial search queries but do not eliminate traffic for authoritative sites.
- Purchase-intent content continues to perform well because users want confidence before buying.
- Traffic is concentrating around high-quality decision-support pages rather than broad informational content.
- Strong brands benefit from increasing direct traffic and repeat visitors.
- Original testing, reviews, comparisons, and user trust remain competitive advantages.

### Why it matters for AI-powered SEO content production

The post demonstrates that AI search rewards authoritative, experience-driven content rather than generic articles. B2B SaaS companies should invest in original research, product comparisons, benchmarks, and unique expertise that AI summaries cannot fully replace.

## Post 2

**Date:** Approximately 3 days ago

**Topic:** Different retrieval systems inside ChatGPT Search

### Summary

Aleyda Solis shares research analyzing ChatGPT Search telemetry across 1,000 prompts. The study suggests that ChatGPT uses multiple retrieval systems—including Labrador, Bright, Oxylabs, and SERP—which retrieve different numbers of URLs, citations, and domains depending on the query.

She argues that AI visibility studies should record which retrieval source generated an answer because different systems behave differently and influence which websites receive citations.

### Key Takeaways

- ChatGPT Search appears to rely on multiple retrieval systems.
- Different retrieval engines produce different citation patterns.
- Citation diversity varies depending on the search source.
- AI visibility research should include retrieval-source analysis.
- Understanding retrieval behavior helps improve AI search optimization.

### Why it matters for AI-powered SEO content production

This post highlights that AI search optimization is becoming more sophisticated than traditional SEO. Content creators need to understand not only rankings but also how different AI retrieval systems discover, evaluate, and cite authoritative sources when generating responses.
