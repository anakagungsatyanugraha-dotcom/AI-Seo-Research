## Post 1

**Date:** Approximately 1 week ago

**Topic:** The SEO, YouTube, and LinkedIn growth framework for B2B SaaS

### Summary

Sam Dunning explains that the most effective inbound growth strategy for B2B SaaS companies combines three channels: SEO/AEO, YouTube, and LinkedIn. He argues that SEO and AI search capture buyers actively searching for solutions, YouTube builds trust through educational content, and LinkedIn strengthens founder branding while supporting paid distribution. Together, these channels generate qualified inbound leads and reduce customer acquisition costs.

### Key Takeaways

- SEO and AI search capture high-intent buyers.
- Educational YouTube content builds authority and trust.
- Founder-led LinkedIn content strengthens brand credibility.
- Combining these three channels creates a sustainable inbound growth engine.
- Multi-channel content marketing outperforms relying on a single platform.

### Why it matters for AI-powered SEO content production

This post demonstrates how AI-powered SEO should fit into a broader B2B SaaS marketing strategy. Rather than treating SEO as an isolated tactic, Sam emphasizes integrating AI search optimization with educational video content and social distribution to maximize reach, authority, and qualified pipeline.

## Post 2

**Date:** Approximately 1 week ago

**Topic:** Three low-cost link building strategies for SaaS SEO

### Summary

Sam Dunning shares three practical approaches to building backlinks without spending heavily on traditional link-building campaigns. He recommends developing strategic partnerships for content collaboration, leveraging previous media coverage to secure stronger backlinks, and appearing on niche podcasts to earn authoritative links and increase brand visibility. He explains that these tactics helped generate inbound demos shortly after launching Breaking B2B.

### Key Takeaways

- Strategic partnerships create mutually beneficial backlink opportunities.
- Existing media mentions can often be converted into stronger SEO assets.
- Podcast guest appearances provide both brand exposure and valuable backlinks.
- High-quality backlinks remain an important ranking factor for SEO and AI search visibility.
- Organic authority compounds over time through consistent relationship building.

### Why it matters for AI-powered SEO content production

Although AI is transforming search, website authority and backlink quality remain essential signals for search engines and AI-powered search systems. This post demonstrates how SaaS companies can strengthen their authority through relationship-driven link building rather than relying on expensive or low-quality SEO tactics.
