## Transcript Summary

In this video, Sam Dunning explains the SEO and AI Search strategy that Breaking B2B implemented for Fibler to help scale the company from approximately $500K ARR toward $1.5M ARR. Rather than focusing on vanity metrics such as traffic volume, the strategy prioritizes high-intent search queries that generate qualified demos and sign-ups.

The framework begins by optimizing existing bottom-of-funnel pages, including comparison pages, alternative pages, pricing pages, and product-focused content. Existing content is updated with stronger structure, richer information, schema markup, customer research, improved metadata, and additional backlinks to improve visibility in both Google Search and AI-powered search systems.

A major emphasis is placed on listicle-based content because AI assistants frequently cite comparison articles when answering commercial queries. The strategy combines publishing authoritative listicles on Fibler's own website with securing placements in external industry listicles to increase brand mentions, backlinks, and AI citations.

The video also discusses creating integration pages, competitor comparison pages, use-case content, and alternative pages targeting low-volume but high-commercial-intent keywords. These pages are designed to capture prospects actively evaluating software solutions rather than casual informational searchers.

Finally, Sam highlights the importance of customer research, transparent pricing, visual product demonstrations, internal linking, authoritative backlinks, podcast appearances, and continuous content updates to improve visibility across Google Search, Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, and other AI search platforms.

## Key Takeaways

- Prioritize high-intent commercial keywords over traffic volume.
- Update existing bottom-of-funnel content before creating new articles.
- Comparison pages and "Best X" listicles perform well in AI search.
- Customer research should drive content creation rather than relying solely on AI-generated text.
- AI search favors structured content, tables, schema markup, and authoritative websites.
- External listicle placements strengthen AI visibility and backlink profiles.
- Integration pages and competitor comparison pages capture qualified buying intent.
- SEO and AI Search should be measured by demos, sign-ups, and pipeline rather than page views alone.
