## Transcript Summary

Kevin Indig explains how artificial intelligence is reshaping search and why marketers should prepare for declining website traffic while focusing on stronger business outcomes. He argues that 2024 may represent the peak year for search traffic as AI Overviews and AI assistants increasingly answer informational queries without requiring users to visit websites.

Rather than viewing this shift as the end of SEO, Kevin describes it as the beginning of a new optimization cycle. He explains that although impressions continue to increase, clicks are declining because AI Overviews satisfy many informational searches directly within Google's interface. However, businesses—particularly B2B SaaS companies—are often maintaining or increasing revenue because the remaining visitors have stronger purchase intent.

Throughout the discussion, Kevin emphasizes that traditional SEO principles still apply. Content freshness, relevance, trust, and brand authority remain the primary drivers of visibility in both Google Search and AI-powered search experiences. At the same time, AI Search introduces new challenges because users now submit much longer, more conversational prompts, creating opportunities for businesses that publish highly specific, intent-driven content.

Kevin also highlights the growing importance of third-party credibility. Since AI systems frequently validate information across sources such as Reddit, YouTube, review platforms, Wikipedia, and industry websites, companies must manage their reputation beyond their own websites. Successful AI Search Optimization therefore requires strong brand authority across the broader web rather than relying solely on traditional on-site SEO.

The interview concludes by exploring how marketers should use AI internally. Kevin recommends applying AI to analyze customer conversations, identify recurring pain points, accelerate research, automate repetitive workflow steps, and build lightweight interactive tools that improve user experience. Rather than replacing marketers, AI enables faster execution while allowing human expertise to remain the strategic advantage.
