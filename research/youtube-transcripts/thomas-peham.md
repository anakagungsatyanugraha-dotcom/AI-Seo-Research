## Transcript Summary

Thomas Peham explains why AI Search represents a fundamental change in digital marketing rather than simply another evolution of traditional SEO. Drawing from his experience building Otterly AI, he discusses how AI assistants such as ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Claude, and Perplexity have transformed customer discovery, requiring marketers to optimize for brand visibility instead of focusing exclusively on website traffic.

The conversation explores how AI-generated answers rely on multiple information sources, including company websites, LinkedIn, YouTube, Reddit, digital PR, and brand mentions. Thomas emphasizes that AI visibility should be viewed as a cross-functional marketing effort involving SEO, content marketing, digital PR, product marketing, and brand teams.

He also explains why traditional SEO metrics such as keyword rankings are becoming less meaningful. Instead, marketers should measure AI visibility through brand mentions, AI citations, referral traffic, self-reported attribution, and overall brand presence within AI-generated answers. Throughout the interview, Thomas stresses continuous experimentation because AI platforms evolve rapidly, meaning today's best practices may change within months.
