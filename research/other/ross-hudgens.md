## Why he is relevant to AI-powered SEO content production for B2B SaaS

Ross Hudgens is the founder and CEO of Siege Media, an SEO and content marketing agency. Siege Media offers GEO/AI search optimization among its services and works with B2B and enterprise clients including Intuit, Zendesk, and Asana.

Ross is the author of an upcoming book, *Generative Engine Optimization: The Definitive Guide to AI SEO*, scheduled to publish with Wiley in Q4 2026. He has spoken publicly about using AI for editing and qualitative content checks in agency production workflows, and about differentiating content with first-party data in an AI-driven landscape.
