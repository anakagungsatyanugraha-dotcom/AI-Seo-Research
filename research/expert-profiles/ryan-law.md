## Why he is relevant to AI-powered SEO content production for B2B SaaS

Ryan Law leads content marketing at Ahrefs, a B2B SaaS company in the SEO tools market. Before Ahrefs, he was CMO at the content marketing agency Animalz and has worked with B2B SaaS companies including Zapier, Clearbit, and Algolia.

He has publicly documented an AI-assisted content production workflow at Ahrefs, including a multi-stage pipeline of AI skills used to research, outline, draft, and optimize blog content. He has discussed this process on the Ahrefs Podcast and in Ahrefs blog coverage of content engineering. His work focuses on applying AI to SEO content workflows while maintaining editorial oversight, which is directly applicable to B2B SaaS content teams scaling organic search production.
