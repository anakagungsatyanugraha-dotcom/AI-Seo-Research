## Why he is relevant to AI-powered SEO content production for B2B SaaS

Thomas Peham is CEO and co-founder of OtterlyAI, an AI search optimization platform used to track brand visibility across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews. Before OtterlyAI, he was VP of Marketing at Storyblok and has worked in B2B SaaS marketing since 2014, including roles at Usersnap and Dynatrace.

He publishes strategic guidance on AI search optimization and generative engine optimization (GEO) for marketing and SEO teams, with a focus on citation tracking, prompt-level measurement, and B2B SaaS use cases. His work bridges SaaS marketing leadership with practical GEO strategy for content and SEO teams.
